Where is the recovery investment coming from?

Five programs funded by the federal government have relieved the need for housing repair, replacement, or relocation for the uninsured. The local administration manage two of them: R3, primarily funded by the federal Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD), and Tu Hogar Renace, with funds from FEMA’s Sheltering and Temporary Essential Power Program (STEP).
